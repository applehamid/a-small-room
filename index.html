A small room

You enter a dimly lit room. There is only a table with a small candle and three glass vials containing shimmering liquids. The only door in the room is the one you entered through.

You approach the table and see a small sign beckoning you to choose one liquid to drink. You take a closer look at the vials.

The first is red and smoky, the second like liquid gold, and the third is an inky black.
