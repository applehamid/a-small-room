A small room

You enter a dimly lit room. There is only a table with a small candle and
three glass vials containing shimmering liquids. The only door in the room
is the one you entered through.

You approach the table and see a small sign beckoning you to choose one
liquid to drink. You take a closer look at the vials.

The first is red and smoky, the second like liquid gold, and the third
is an inky black.

You feel a warmth radiate throughout your muscles. You clench your fists and
feel an overwhelming sense of power.

A door appears across the room and you walk towards it, flinging the door
open unintentionally with your newfound strength...

You feel a lightness float throughout your body.

A door appears across the room and you walk towards it, your feet gliding on the
floor. The door opens quietly as you get closer to it, without you lifting a finger...

You feel a cold chill run through you and start to shiver uncontrollably.

You fall to the floor as the light from the candle grows dimmer and your eyes close...

An underground quarry

The door opens into a small underground quarry. Your feet crunch in stone gravel
as you make your way through the room.

You hear a low grumbling sound, but you can barely see anything.

Your match illuminates the the ground at your feet to reveal an assortment of tools.

Their handles look worn but they seem usable...

You pick up the mallet and continue to make your way through the quarry.
You feel smaller stones cascade down the quarry walls and land around your feet.

The grumbling is getting louder...

You come to a stone door with strange carvings on it. It looks like it can be pushed open.

You pick up the axe and continue to make your way through the quarry.
You feel smaller stones cascade down the quarry walls and land around your feet.

The grumbling is getting louder...

You come to a stone door with strange carvings on it. It looks like it can be pushed open.

You pick up the shovel and continue to make your way through the quarry.
You feel smaller stones cascade down the quarry walls and land around your feet.

The grumbling is getting louder...

You come to a stone door with strange carvings on it. It looks like it can be pushed open.

A stone temple

The stone door moves with a cloud of dust into what looks like a place of worship.
The same carvings on the door are present on the walls and columns around a
round dais in the center of the room.

The grumbling is louder than ever as you set eyes on a large stone formation that
appears to be moving slightly.

A great golem awakens as you walk into the temple and starts to move
towards you...

You grasp your weapon and take a swing as the golem lunges towards you.
The weapon sings as metal hits stone and it sinks in between the great golem's eyes.

The golem groans loudly before crumbling into a pile of stone at your feet.

The grumbling in the temple subsides.
